How to Play?

1️⃣ Enter players names. Spin to choose a random host.

2️⃣ Host generates a question to ask players. Players answer the question honestly on a piece of paper.

3️⃣ Then give their papers to the host. Who randomly reads them aloud anonymously.

4️⃣ Players guess who gave each answer. Handing in their guesses to the host.

5️⃣ Now the host tallies up everyones correct answers for points, after 5 rounds, the person with most points wins.
